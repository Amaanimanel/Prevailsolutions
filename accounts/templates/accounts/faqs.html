At Prevail Solutions we provide every sort of employment; from temporary, contractual to permanent employees for all kinds of job descriptions

What is PREVAIL SOLUTIONS about?

There is nothing more valuable and satisfying than finding quality candidates for the right job. We are dedicated and committed to helping you build a workforce of excellence.

At Prevail Solutions we provide recruitment, training, and consulting services that are designed to meet the specific needs of employers. We match the employers with qualified candidates after reviewing their needs, nature of work, skill set and cost-effectiveness. At Prevail Solutions we provide every sort of employment, temporary or contractual services on certain conditions and provide permanent staff demand in IT, Accounting and Finance, Healthcare, Logistics, Reprographics, General Business, Office Administration and Management We are driven by a passion to see you succeed.

What type of TRAINING AND CONSULTING do we provide?

Prevail Solutions provides first class training and consulting services to our clients that are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients.

Training services are offered to cover topics in Healthcare, Leadership, Occupational and Physical Therapy, Accounting and Finance, with Consulting services provided for new business start-ups, life coaching, mental health community-based programs, workplace redesign to reduce injury and promote staff retention and corporate wellness.

The company is situated in the Crofton area of Maryland, where the head office operates from. We offer our clients quality and well qualified candidates to meet their staffing needs and their budget. We offer flexibility for our clients in different sectors to help them find suitable candidates.

How do we serve EMPLOYERS?

At Prevail Solutions we work in collaboration with employers to provide different packages in recruitment, training and consulting services that are tailored to meet the specific needs of your company.

We provide every sort of employment temporary or contractual services on certain conditions and provide permanent staff on demand. We offer tailored Training in healthcare, accounting, finance, and corporate wellness programs. Our Consulting services are geared towards staff development, compliance, company growth and team building.
